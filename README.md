# Strategic Data Audit: Eniac x Magist Fusion 📊🚀

## 📋 Executive Summary
This project presents a comprehensive strategic audit for **Eniac**, a Spanish Apple Premium Reseller, evaluating the potential acquisition of **Magist**, a prominent Brazilian e-commerce marketplace.

The final recommendation is a **"Conditional YES"**. While Magist offers significant scalability within the São Paulo tech hub, the audit revealed critical infrastructure gaps and security risks across Brazil's broader regions. A successful merger is only recommended if followed by a structured 3-phase roadmap to align Magist’s marketplace model with Eniac’s premium brand standards.

## 🔍 Key Strategic Insights

### 1. Portfolio Mismatch & Brand Alignment 🛍️
Despite being a "tech" marketplace, high-end electronics account for only **21% of revenue**. The core assortment consists of low-cost items (<100€). For Eniac to succeed, a strategic shift toward premium segment positioning within the platform is mandatory, as Eniac's current focus remains a niche in this ecosystem.

### 2. The Punctuality Illusion ⏱️
Magist reports a **93% on-time delivery rate**, but the data audit shows this is an illusion based on artificially extended system estimates (25–30 days). The actual delivery time for tech products is **13 days**—far too slow for a premium customer experience.

### 3. "Two-Speed Brazil": The Geospatial Performance Gap 🗺️
The analysis identifies a stark contrast in operational efficiency:
* **São Paulo (The Powerhouse):** Hosts 59% of sellers; average delivery in **9 days**.
* **The Northern Gap:** Customers in the North/Northeast face wait times of **23–27 days**. 
* **Security Risk:** Long-haul routes to the North are compromised by extreme security risks, with approximately **22,000 cargo thefts per year**.

---

## 🗺️ Strategic Roadmap
To mitigate risks and ensure ROI, the fusion must follow these phases:
1.  **Phased Expansion:** Initial focus exclusively on the **Sudeste/South** regions to leverage existing efficiency.
2.  **Regional Fulfillment:** Construction of dedicated logistics hubs in the North to bypass high-risk transit routes.
3.  **Service Tiering:** Implementation of a **Premium Tier** (5–7 days delivery) vs. a **Standard Tier** (15–20 days) to manage customer expectations.
